About Nepal

Nepal, officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, is a landlocked country in South Asia. It is bordered by China to the north and north-east, India to the south and south-east, and Bhutan to the west. Nepal is the 72nd largest country in the world by area and the 104th largest by population. The capital and largest city is Kathmandu.
